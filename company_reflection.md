**Why do you think Focus Bear was created?**
- Focus Bear was created to help people enhance focus and productivity while working by blocking distractions. It specifically targets the people who are diagnosed with ADHD, which is a disorder that greatly affects one's ability to focus and finish tasks consistently.

**What problem is Focus Bear solving?**
- Focus Bear is solving the problem of people, specifically those with ADHD, struggling to focus and finish tasks consistently due to distractions. It provides a solution that helps them stay on track and complete their work effectively by blocking distractions and promoting healthy habits to enhance focus and consistency.

**Why do you think this mission is important?**
- This mission of Focus Bear is important because it addresses an ongoing increasing problem of people who have ADHD and are struggling to focus and finish tasks consistently due to distractions. It also promotes a healthier and more productive work environment for everyone.
